Genius Annotation

1 Countributor

Initially, Rumi was deeply ashamed of her demon heritage and her demon patterns were a reminder of who she really was inside. She grew to hate her demon identity and tried to cover it no matter the cost.

However, after her identity was exposed during the Idol Awards, she finally learnt to accept her true self and embrace the imperfections that made her who she is now.
